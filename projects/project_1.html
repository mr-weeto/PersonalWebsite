Music Project: Go!Gettum

As the front man of the band Go!gettum, I was able to excercise soo many of the skill sets I have developed over the years. Singing, guitar playing, song writing, and digital art. I learned so much. I was able to enjoy spending so much time dedicated to practicing my instrument and honing the sound of our music, focusing not only on the playing but also the tone of the instrument and the cohesion of the band sonically. I learned to collaborate more deeply with other musicians when song writing and fleshing out performances. I got to grow alongside the friends I had for years and developed new friendships. Unfortunatly it can be difficult to have realistic expections regarding the relationship between results and effort in a creative project. As a result, the most significant lesson was how to deal with disappointment. After some honest conversations with fellow band mates about realistic expectations regarding time spent preacticing and rehearsing performances, It became apparent that it was time to walk away from something that just wasn't working.
