I learned how to harness team memebers individual strengths as well as navigate issues with individuals trying to take over others responsibilities. I was introduced to working with LLM API's and proper storage of api keys, as well as an introduction to RAG with large language models. Our teams combined skill, creativity, and dedication brought us to victory over the other team in the competition.
